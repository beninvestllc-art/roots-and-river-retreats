Our Services

Tourism Services

We specialize in offering unique and immersive eco-tourism experiences in Uganda and Tanzania. Our tours include captivating gorilla trekking, thrilling safaris, and nature-based adventures. We work with local guides and communities to ensure an authentic and sustainable travel experience, directly benefiting the people and places you visit.

Real Estate & Investment

Our real estate arm focuses on developing sustainable guesthouses and hotel projects in prime tourist locations. These ventures are designed for both environmental sustainability and strong financial returns. We offer a clear pathway for investors to get involved, providing opportunities to become a part of our eco-friendly development projects and share in their success.
