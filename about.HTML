Our Vision

Our Story

Founded by Tommy James, Roots & River Retreats was born from a passion for sustainable development and a deep appreciation for the unique landscapes and cultures of East Africa. Our vision is to create profitable opportunities for investors that also contribute positively to the communities and environment we operate in. We are a venture by **Beneficial Investments International LLC**, a company dedicated to responsible investment.

Our Mission & Vision

Our mission is to empower sustainable tourism and real estate development in Uganda and Tanzania. We are committed to an eco-friendly approach, ensuring our projects respect the natural ecosystems and support local communities through employment and partnerships. Our vision is to be a leading force in responsible investment, proving that financial success and social responsibility can go hand-in-hand.

Our Focus

We specialize in identifying and developing projects that blend financial viability with environmental stewardship. Our work is concentrated on the creation of eco-friendly guesthouses and hotels near major tourist attractions. By focusing on sustainable practices, we aim to create long-term value for our investors and a lasting legacy for the region.
